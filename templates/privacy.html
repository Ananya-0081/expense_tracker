Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

At TrackIt, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information the TrackIt personal expense tracking application (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly to us. This includes account details such as your name, email address, and password when you register, as well as the financial information you choose to enter — expenses, income, categories, budgets, dates, and notes. We may also collect limited technical data, such as your browser type and general usage patterns, to help us keep the Service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service. This means displaying your spending history and summaries back to you, generating reports and analytics, sending important account or bill reminders, and maintaining the security of your account. We do not sell your personal financial data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is associated with your account. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. While no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, we work to protect your information and to retain it only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may delete your data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

The Service may rely on trusted third-party providers for hosting, fonts, icons, and similar infrastructure. These providers only process the limited data necessary to perform their functions and are expected to handle it responsibly. We do not share your personal financial information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the Service. We may update this policy from time to time, and when we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
